Album Review (Mahdi Hasan)

This album, by Kendrick Lamar, is a fan favorite for some.
I, personally, don't listen to music much, but I thought the songs in this album was
somewhat enjoyable. The melody and instrumentation was very unconventioal and a bit lazy, and the lyrics are a bit controversial and quite explicit.
Overall, though, the album still sounded amazing and I would definetely recommend it to anyone thats looking into rap.
