Talking Chipmunks

TALKING CHIPMUNKS?!?!

Recently, there's been an outbreak of talking chipmunks. In southern California, 14-year old Jamal Skibidi was walking home from school, where he encountered a herd of chipmunks communicating with each other. Jamal was astonished. He was eavesdropping in and heard them plotting to take over the human race. He immediately ran to the nearest police station to report what he heard. Apparently, this isn't the first case of this. People from cities like Cairo, Milan and Sydney have also been saying the same thing. IS THE HUMAN RACE REALLY IN DANGER?!?!
